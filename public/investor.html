Digital Clearance Code System Technology

Every file uploaded to DCCS receives a unique Digital Clearance Code that creates an unbreakable chain of ownership. This cryptographic fingerprint is permanent, publicly verifiable, and legally binding.

Upload & Fingerprint

When creators upload content, the system generates a unique cryptographic fingerprint using advanced audio/visual analysis. This fingerprint is stored permanently and cannot be altered.

Instant Certificate

Within seconds, creators receive a DCCS Certificate with a unique clearance code. This certificate includes timestamp, fingerprint hash, and creator identity verification.

Public Verification

Anyone can verify ownership by entering the clearance code on our public portal. The system instantly confirms authenticity, timestamp, and original creator.

Legal Protection

DCCS certificates serve as legal proof of creation date and ownership. Courts worldwide recognize cryptographic timestamps as valid evidence.
