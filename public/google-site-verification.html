Google Search Console Verification Guide

Follow these steps to verify your site and get indexed on Google.

Step 1: Go to Google Search Console

Visit Google Search Console and sign in with your Google account.

Step 2: Add Your Property

Click "Add Property" and enter:

https://dccsverify.com

Step 3: Choose Verification Method

Select "HTML tag" method. Google will give you a meta tag like:

<meta name="google-site-verification" content="YOUR-CODE-HERE" />

Copy the entire code they provide.

Step 4: Add the Tag

Send the verification code to your developer to add it to the site's <head> section.

Alternatively, you can download an HTML verification file and upload it to your site root.

Step 5: Submit Your Sitemap

Once verified, go to Sitemaps in the left menu and submit:

https://dccsverify.com/sitemap.xml

Step 6: Request Indexing

Use the URL Inspection tool to request indexing for your main pages:

  • https://dccsverify.com/
  • https://dccsverify.com/music
  • https://dccsverify.com/video
  • https://dccsverify.com/podcast
  • https://dccsverify.com/booking

Already Configured:

  • Sitemap.xml with 23 pages
  • Robots.txt properly configured
  • Mobile-friendly responsive design
  • Fast page load times
  • Clean, indexable URLs
Note: Indexing typically takes 1-3 days for first results, and 1-2 weeks for complete site indexing.

Optional: Remove Old Site from Google

If you have an old version in Google search results:

  1. Go to Removals in Search Console
  2. Click "New Request"
  3. Enter the old URLs to remove
  4. Submit (processes in 24-48 hours)
